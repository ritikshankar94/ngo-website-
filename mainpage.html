About
In 2021, Smriti Pandey, Ritik Shankar, Anshu Kumari and Piyush Tiwari cofounded the Care for poor foundation in India, a nonprofit that works for betterment of underprivileged and also awares them regarding various issues.
It directly benefits over 5,00,000 children and their families till now, through more than 100 live welfare projects on education, healthcare, livelihood and women empowerment, in over 500 villages and slums across several states in India.

.



Ideology

We together as society need to join hands to bring a change in lives of the under priviledged. We need to closely understand the problems and difficulty they face in their daily lives. In response to the Covid-19 crisis, India has imposed one of the longest and strictest lockdowns anywhere in the world. While the lockdown may have been effective in managing the spread of Covid-19 cases, it severly impacted livlihood of many, most of them being needy.
We saw many individuals stepping forward to help but they didn't exactly know how to contribute for this cause. This envoked a feeling of concern in us and hence we decided to provide them a platform to tackle the situation. Subsequently Care for poor was established.
